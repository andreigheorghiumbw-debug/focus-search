## Detailed Description
Tired of Google results filled with AI-generated 'slop', Pinterest boards, and SEO content farms? FocusSearch is your one-click escape back to a high-signal web.

### Key Features:
- 🚫 **The Slop Killer:** Instantly excludes domains like Pinterest, Quora, and known content farms.
- 🤖 **AI Detox:** Automatically triggers Google's 'Classic Web' view (udm=14), hiding AI Overviews and Shopping widgets.
- 👥 **Human-First Filters:** Dedicated search buttons for Reddit, forums, and technical engineering sites.
- 🔒 **Privacy-First:** We don't track you. We don't save your searches. We just clean the UI.
